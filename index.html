  3. How do you feel about waiting times at hospitals?

    • Much longer than we would like
    • Backlog of operating, ability to see patients for appointments, meet patient desires and expectations and requirements

  4. Are hospital staff overworked? Why/why not?

    • Yes, of course they are
    • Understaffed - people come in who are often not fit to work because they don't want to let anyone down (patients and staff alike)
    • Poor managerial economies of scale
    • Managers have different motives than clinicians - managers are saving money while clinicians want to deliver the absolute best care
    • Different but equal pressures for members of staff in term of clinic
